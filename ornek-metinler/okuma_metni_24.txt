MAHATMA GANDHI
Mahatma Gandhi was born in India in 1869. He became one of his
country's leaders. Life was difficult when he was young. He got married at
the age of thirteen; a common custom in India at that time. He studied law at
the university. He went to London and became a lawyer. Then he returned to
his country. A few years later, he went to work in South Africa. He
organised demonstrations* and they sent him to prison several times. Twenty
years later, he returned to India. There, he worked hard to help the poor. He
played a very important role in the independence* of India. A fanatic killed
him in 1948.
