AMY'S RESTAURANT
Amy's Restaurant is an example of a good place to eat in Greenwich
Village. It's a perfect place for students to eat lunch and dinner. It's clean, the
service is fast and the prices are reasonable. Amy's has a different special
every day. The special includes fish or meat, vegetables, a mixed salad, bread
and butter, dessert and coffee or tea - all for $3.45. The special and the other
items on Amy's menu are always excellent for the prices.
