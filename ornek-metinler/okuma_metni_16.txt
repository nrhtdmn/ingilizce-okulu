YOUNG LOVE AT THE CAVE
Sarah Jones, Pete Parsons and Bette Lord work in Manchester.
Sarah is a secretary. Pete drives a taxi and Bette teaches music. But
tonight they are in London. They're playing at The Cave. Who are
they? They're the new band YOUNG LOVE. From Monday to Friday
they work in Manchester. At the weekend they sing in pubs and
discos. They've got two jobs and they work very hard. Sarah Jones
says, 'It isn't easy. I'm very busy. My office opens at 9.00 and I
usually work from 9.00 to 5.00. I'm not working today because we're
singing tonight in London'.
YOUNG LOVE are playing at The Cave, Lower Harrington St, at
8.30. Tickets are £4.
