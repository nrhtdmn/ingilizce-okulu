THE ĞARSTON FAMILY
Mr. and Mrs. Garston live in a small village near London. They've
got two dogs. Their names are Bruce and Spot. The dogs eat meat,
biscuits and special dog food. Mr. Garston buys their food in London
because the shops in their village don't sell dog food. The Garstons
have got neighbours, too, but they don't like Bruce and Spot because
they've got a cat. The postman doesn't like the dogs, either.
Mr. Garston goes to London every day because he works there. He
comes home at six. The dogs always wait for him. They stand near the
door and bark. They are happy because they go for a long walk in the
evening with Mr. Garston.
